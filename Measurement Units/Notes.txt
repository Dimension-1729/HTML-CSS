cm is used for properties which deal with shapes and sizes or spacing of elements. 
 1 cm = 37.8px

 mm used when content is to be displayed on any printing media platform, as length size expressed with this unit is very precise
 1mm = 1/10 = 0.1cm = 3.78px

inch : Also for print output.
1 inch = 96px = 2.54cm

pica(pc) : is also another typography unit for printing content on a paper and is 1/6th of an inch
1pc = 16 px
